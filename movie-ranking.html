The Best Movies According to Ariana

My top 3 movies of all-time

Shutter Island

Shutter Island is a psychological thriller that exists as both a 2003 novel by Dennis Lehane and a highly successful 2010 film adaptation directed by Martin Scorsese. Both versions center on a U.S. Marshal's investigation at an isolated, fortress-like asylum that leads to a mind-bending twist about his own identity and reality.

Midsommar

A young American couple, their relationship foundering, travel to a Swedish midsummer festival where a seemingly pastoral paradise transforms into a sinister, dread-soaked nightmare as the locals reveal their terrifying agenda.
